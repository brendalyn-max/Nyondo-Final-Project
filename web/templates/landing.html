Designed to completely eliminate manual documentation deficits through optimized architectural processing layers.

1. Inventory Integrity

Protects retail revenue margins via rigid model validation checks (Rule #6), ensuring product selling values can never sit below baseline acquisition unit costs.

Enforces national accounting compliance parameters natively at database layers, checking for valid Ugandan phone configurations and unique 14-character NIRA National IDs (NIN).

Calculates geographical delivery distribution charges automatically during order processing, linking distance inputs directly with dynamic free-shipping threshold logic values.
